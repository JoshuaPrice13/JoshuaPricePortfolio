About Me

I’ve been surrounded by computers for as long as I can remember. There’s even a photo of me as a toddler barely tall enough to reach the desk, clicking through puzzle games with a mouse. But my journey into the technical side of computing truly began when I started recreating my father’s calculator games — and quickly leveled up to building full 3D simulations on the desktop.

Today, I'm pursuing a Bachelor’s in Computer Science at Oklahoma State University, developing a strong foundation in software engineering with languages like Java, C++, and C#. I’ve worked on everything from drone controllers in Java to complete video games in Unity using C# — all while diving into advanced math like Vector Calculus.

As a proud Native American, I'm passionate about representation in STEM. Through my involvement with the American Indian Science and Engineering Society, I aim to encourage and empower future Native students to pursue technical fields — just like I did.
